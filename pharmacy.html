Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Ph.)

Background

Diploma in Pharmacy is a three year programme of CTEVT which aims at preparing pharmacy assistance with all scientific knowledge, technical competencies and human understanding so as to discourage practice of quack which is badly affecting the health of people. To prevent the drug resistance and the disaster from wrong medicine/chemical practice, the pharmacy assistants will be mainly responsible to.

Objectives

To prepare diploma level pharmacy assistant
To prepare supportive technical human resource for pharmaceutical Manufacturing quality control & drug control.
To prepare supportive technical human resource for rational dispensing of drugs in hospital pharmacy & compounding extemporaneous preparations.

Scope of Pharmacy

  • Continuing education in different universities in Nepal, Bangladesh, India and abroad.
  • Opening retail pharmacy shop in municipality areas.
  • Getting job in government & private hospitals pharmacies.
  • Working in the community pharmacy.
  • In Pharmaceutical Industries.
  • In educational institutional as instructor in different NGO's /INGO's.
  • In quality Control laboratories as an analyst.
