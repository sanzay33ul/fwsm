About Shyam Sundar Jha

Mr.Shyam Sundar Jha

Shyam Sundar Jha, Campus Chief of Farwest School of Medicine, holds a Bachelor of Medical Laboratory Technology (BMLT) degree. With over 15 years of experience in educational administration, he is dedicated to fostering academic excellence and an inclusive campus culture. Known for his collaborative leadership, Mr. Jha has significantly enhanced student services and innovative programs at Farwest School of Medicine.

About Prakash Mahara

Mr.Prakash Mahara

Prakash Mahara is the Pharmacy Coordinator at Farwest School of Medicine, holding a Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) degree. With extensive experience in pharmaceutical education, he is committed to high academic standards and innovative teaching. Mr. Mahara works closely with faculty and students to enhance the curriculum and prepare graduates for successful careers in pharmacy. His dedication to student success and professional development is highly valued at Farwest School of Medicine.

About Dharmanand Joshi

Mr.Dharmand Joshi

Dharmanand Joshi is the Coordinator of General Medicine at Farwest School of Medicine, holding a Bachelor in Public Health (BPH) degree. With a strong background in public health and medical education, he is dedicated to maintaining high academic standards and innovative teaching. Mr. Joshi collaborates closely with faculty and students to enhance the curriculum and prepare graduates for successful careers in general medicine. His commitment to student success and professional development is highly valued at Farwest School of Medicine.
